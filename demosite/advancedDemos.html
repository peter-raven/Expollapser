WCAG compliance

In expollapser's default setting, the header will be made clickable and will thereby work as the toggler. In some cases (for instance to follow WCAG guidelines, keyboard navigation, etc), you may want only real (a href) links inside the header to work as togglers. Use the toggler setting headerLinks for this.
